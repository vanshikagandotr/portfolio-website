Wedding Pavilion

Fall 2016

Located on the coast of Lake Eerie, this design exhibits a wedding pavilion set amidst hedges and overlooking views on the banks of Lake Eerie. Conceptually designed around the ideas of experientiality, the wedding pavilion enjoys spectacular views of the lake, tying together nature and matrimony.

Digital Media

Fall 2016

These drawings make use of softwares such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign and Rhino to create compelling images that preview the different forms of representation that architects make use of. Using these softwares exclusively, and sometimes in conjunction, generates a series of interesting images, including hybrids that also make use of hand drawings.

Motion Model

Spring 2017

Starting with multiple sectional drawings of a household kitchen tool, this study developed into one that required the construction of a model that displayed motion. The model was developed through sectional drawings and was made in collaboration with partner Harry BandSaw.
